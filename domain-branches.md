# Domain branch ideas

Directions that **share DNA with BetterCals** (personal labs, metabolism, behavior, education) but could become **their own products**, **deep verticals**, or **partnership tracks**. Not committed work—use to brainstorm spin-offs, content series, or long-term bets.

For **who the current app serves**, see `audience.md`. For **what we might build next in-repo**, see `implementations.md`. For **ML architecture**, see `logic.md`.

---

## Clinical & care adjacent

- **Peri-op or procedure prep** — Prehab nutrition + lab targets before elective surgery (albumin, Hb, glucose stability); discharge checklists.
- **Oncology supportive care** — Weight trajectory, appetite, basic labs; strictly adjunct to oncology teams, not staging.
- **Women’s health lifecycle** — Iron/B12/D ferritin arcs across pregnancy and postpartum; thyroid panels tied to energy and weight narratives.
- **Pediatric family mode** — Parent-managed profiles with age-appropriate ranges and growth context (heavy regulation; often a separate product).

---

## Performance & longevity

- **Endurance sport fueling** — Glycogen, sweat sodium estimates, race-week carb load vs. lab glucose/HbA1c and lipids.
- **Strength sport periodization** — Creatine kinase trends, protein targets vs. eGFR; “bulk/cut” tied to labs.
- **Sleep-debt and metabolism** — Simple models linking sleep surveys to insulin sensitivity language (not diagnosis).
- **“Biological age” scorecards** — Composite indices from labs + lifestyle; always framed as experimental/educational.

---

## Behavior, food, and environment

- **Alcohol and liver stack** — GGT, AST/ALT, triglycerides with cutback plans and check-in cadence.
- **Anti-inflammatory trials** — hs-CRP before/after structured diet windows; journaling + second lab.
- **Seed-oil / omega-3 literacy** — Omega-3 index as a paid add-on lab channel integration (partnership play).
- **Workplace wellness** — Anonymous cohort dashboards for employers (privacy-heavy, B2B).

---

## Data, trust, and infrastructure

- **Personal health record (PHR) export hub** — One-click bundles: FHIR, CCDA, PDF for any downstream clinician tool.
- **Research opt-in** — De-identified longitudinal panels for population studies (consent UX is the product).
- **Second-opinion marketplace** — Hand off structured package to telehealth clinicians (marketplace, not clinical core).
- **Lab price transparency** — “Order this panel” links with regional cash pricing (affiliate or public good).

---

## Education & media

- **Marker-of-the-week** — Short explainers + recipes tied to one analyte (SEO + trust).
- **Clinician-facing glossary** — Same engine, different copy pack for nurses and PAs.
- **School / university module** — Teaching metabolic math and ASCVD framing without storing PHI.

---

## Hardware & offline

- **Printed “lab wallet”** — QR to a read-only snapshot for elderly users or low-tech caregivers.
- **Kiosk or clinic waiting-room mode** — Ephemeral session, no account, print summary.

---

## How to use this list

- **Product:** Pick one branch, scope an MVP that reuses your types + calculation style but new UX.
- **Content:** Each bullet can become a landing page or newsletter arc without shipping code.
- **Partnerships:** B2B rows (employers, clinics, research) usually need a one-pager; this file seeds those narratives.

Prune or move items into `implementations.md` when they become real backlog.
